About Student Planner

Why this Planner?

This student planner is designed to help you organize your academic and personal life in a simple and visually friendly way. Whether you're preparing for exams, managing daily chores, or planning outdoor activities, this tool is here to keep everything in one place.

What You Can Do

Navigate through different sections like Study Time, Exam Preparation, Outdoor Activities, and Daily Chores. Each section includes an interactive to-do list to help you stay productive and motivated!
